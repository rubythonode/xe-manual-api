\subsection{Detailed Description}
H\+T\+M\+L \hyperlink{classPurifier}{Purifier}'s internal representation of a U\+R\+I. \begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
Internal data-\/structures are completely escaped. If the data needs to be used in a non-\/\+U\+R\+I context (which is very unlikely), be sure to decode it first. The U\+R\+I may not necessarily be well-\/formed until \hyperlink{classHTMLPurifier__URI_a6c6bbc63988cd266c5f264cffce66c55}{validate()} is called. 
\end{DoxyNote}
