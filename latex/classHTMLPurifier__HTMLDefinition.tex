\subsection{Detailed Description}
Definition of the purified H\+T\+M\+L that describes allowed children, attributes, and many other things.

Conventions\+:

All member variables that are prefixed with info (including the main \$info array) are used by H\+T\+M\+L \hyperlink{classPurifier}{Purifier} internals and should not be directly edited when customizing the H\+T\+M\+L\+Definition. They can usually be set via configuration directives or custom modules.

On the other hand, member variables without the info prefix are used internally by the H\+T\+M\+L\+Definition and M\+U\+S\+T N\+O\+T be used by other H\+T\+M\+L \hyperlink{classPurifier}{Purifier} internals. Many of them, however, are public, and may be edited by userspace code to tweak the behavior of H\+T\+M\+L\+Definition.
